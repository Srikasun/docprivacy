Privacy Policy for DocXpress

Last Updated: March 13, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to DocXpress ("we", "us", "our" or "Company"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices and your rights regarding information we collect through our mobile application and services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password (hashed and encrypted)
  • User Behavior: App ratings, feature usage, and interaction data
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, IP address, app version
  • Files You Process: Documents you convert using our service (temporarily stored for processing)
  • Log Data: Timestamps, request types, error information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

  • Creating and managing your account
  • Providing document conversion services
  • Improving app features and user experience
  • Sending important notifications about account or service changes
  • Analyzing app performance and fixing issues
  • Complying with legal obligations

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Database: User account data stored on MongoDB Atlas with encryption at rest
  • Authentication: Passwords hashed with bcryptjs (never stored in plain text)
  • Transmission: All data transmitted over HTTPS/SSL encryption
  • Tokens: JWT authentication tokens with 7-day expiration
  • Converted Files: Immediately deleted from our servers after download. We do not retain copies of your converted documents

5. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Temporary Files: Deleted immediately after download or processing. No files are permanently stored on our servers
  • App Ratings: Retained for analytics and service improvement
  • Log Data: Retained for up to 90 days for security purposes

6. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. We may share data only when:

  • Required by law or legal process
  • Necessary to protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • With your explicit consent
  • With service providers who process data on our behalf (all under confidentiality agreements)

7. Children's Privacy

DocXpress does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information immediately and terminate the child's account.

8. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information through app settings
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt-out: Disable notifications in app settings
  • Export: Request your data in portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

9. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • MongoDB Atlas: Cloud database provider (privacy policy: mongodb.com/legal/privacy)
  • Render.com: Backend hosting (privacy policy: render.com/privacy)
  • Google Play Services: For app distribution and analytics

10. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be notified through the app or email. Your continued use of DocXpress after changes means you accept the updated policy.

11. Your Consent

By using DocXpress, you consent to our Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.
